The Challenge

The client needed to deploy hundreds of wireless sensor nodes across an industrial facility to monitor temperature, humidity, and vibration in real time. The nodes had to connect to an existing WiFi 6 infrastructure, last at least 12 months on a battery charge in low-activity mode, and cost under $15 in BOM at 500-unit volumes.

The design also needed to support USB-C charging and OTA firmware updates over WiFi â€” without a separate programming interface, since the nodes would be sealed once deployed.
