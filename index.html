About

The Engineer Behind MicroCore

I'm a hardware electronics engineer with 5+ years of experience taking products from concept all the way to production.

My background spans IoT systems, RF design, power electronics, motor control, sensor interfaces, and wireless communication — essentially the full breadth of modern hardware design.

I started MicroCore Electronics because I kept seeing the same problem: great ideas stuck in development hell because the engineering process was fragmented. My approach is different — I stay with the project from the first whiteboard sketch to the final production run.

Whether you're a startup building your first hardware product or an experienced team that needs extra bandwidth, I deliver the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.
