2.4GHz RF Receiver Chain

Signal chain for SDR integration: band-pass filter (2340–2530MHz), RF limiter, low-noise amplifier (22dB gain), and output limiter. Full link budget analysis from 1m to 500m range. Enabled reliable long-range wireless detection without costly off-the-shelf RF modules.

Jetson Orin Carrier Board

Custom carrier board for NVIDIA Jetson Orin. PCIe x4 NVMe SSD, 4-port USB 3.0 hub, HDMI output, multi-rail power management at 3.3V/4.7A. Full high-speed signal integrity layout. Saved the client months of development time vs. adapting a generic carrier board.

BLE Biopotential Recorder

8-channel biopotential acquisition system using ADS1298 analog front-end and nRF5340 dual-core BLE SoC. Runs on 2x AA batteries with dual boost converters. Wireless data streaming. Delivered a fully wireless, battery-powered medical-grade device ready for clinical trials.

WiFi 6 IoT Sensor Platform

Compact IoT device built around ESP32-C6 (WiFi 6 + BLE 5). USB-C with BQ24232 Li-Ion charger, TPS63021 buck/boost for stable 3.3V across full battery range. Designed for long-term field deployment with full USB-C charging and minimal BOM cost.

Industrial STM32 Controller

Modular field controller based on STM32G474 (Cortex-M4, 170MHz). RS485 communication via ADM1490E transceiver, extensive GPIO, designed for harsh industrial environments. Modular architecture allowed the client to reuse the platform across multiple product variants.

Wireless Solenoid Controller

BLE-based control module (nRF5340) driving solenoid valves via 74HCT595 shift register. SPI pressure sensing, battery-powered with LDO regulation. Eliminated wiring harness complexity in a multi-node field system, cutting installation time significantly.
